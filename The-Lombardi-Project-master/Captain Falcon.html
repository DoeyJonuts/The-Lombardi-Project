Captain Falcon

Guess Man got ya down?
3 Rules for the Matchup


1. High lasers to beat Nair, then turn your back for bairs and uptilts.
2. Don't respect Falcon's shield - late nair shine grab fthrow, late aerial waveshine back, late nair dash back. Really only worried about Stomp OOS, shieldgrab, and roll.
3. Falcons will almost always nair when given the chance, SDI nair at low percent, its reactable when you get hit as you laser. Essentially, Falcon's aerials at low percent into a grab aren't always real, you have counterplay.

Getting caught by Nair

Falcon's Nair will often catch you mid-laser and combo into a grab, but you can react mid-Nair to prevent Falcon from getting a follow-up.

Counterplay

On reaction to your SHL getting Naired, you can SDI and ASDI away for the second hit of Nair. This lets you dodge an immediate grab and whiff punish.
